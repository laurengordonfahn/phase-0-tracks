How to Pack the Kitchen

Keep these addages in mind: When we love something let it go. When we love something pack it with care. When we love someone else we don't weight them down with our stuff!

  1. Pack Matching dishes first
  2. Pack flatware in the box with the plastic tuber ware to keep weight reasonable
  3. Pack Spices with around the plates
  4. Pack teas around the serving bowls
  5. Donate everything that you haven't used in two years, including cook books, its all online

Tips to Find Happiness In Packing-Up

  • Get ride of things you haven't thought about in years
  • Pair like with Like
  • Give to a good organization you can stand behind
  • Think about how much less you will need to unpack and organize in your new home
  • Play happy Packing Music
